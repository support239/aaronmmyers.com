Navigating Relationship Friction

Most problems aren't caused by a lack of love, but by a breakdown in how that love is expressed and how actions are interpreted.

Love Languages

We often give love the way we want to receive it. By identifying your partner's specific language, you ensure your efforts are felt and appreciated.

Conation

This is the "doing" part of the mind. Understanding your partner’s natural method of striving helps you stop fighting their instincts and start working with them.

Expectation

Friction often lives in the gap between what we expect and what we communicate. Bridging this gap is essential for long-term peace.
